Members

Aiven Dawood

An electrical engineer with a focus on communication, networking, and embedded programming, Aiven Dawood has led the project SAR Bumble Bees which was designed to help find victims trapped underneath rubble and debris during the event of an earthquake.

Victor Munguia

As a computer engineer and a fanatic of electrical components, Victor Munguia has supported the project by proposing and managing the quality of the components used to make the sensor package and achieve the desired objective of locating victims. In addition, he plays an important role as the chief programmer for the project, creator of interfaces, and embedded programming.
