Aiven Dawood

An electrical engineer with a focus on communication, networking, and embedded programming, Aiven Dawood has led the project SAR Bumble Bees which was designed to help find victims trapped underneath rubble and debris during the event of an earthquake.

In addition, he plays an important role as the chief programmer for the project, creator of interfaces, and embedded programming.

Rachelle Ramos
